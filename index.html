Lease structure — NN, not absolute NNN. Per the executed lease, the landlord (ShopOne Centers REIT, private) is responsible for foundation, structure, and roof. With 2026 new construction and transferable builder's warranties in place, these are not onerous near-term obligations — a roof warranty on a new building typically runs 20+ years, and structural issues on new construction are rare. The distinction is real and should be understood, but it is not necessarily a material risk given the age and condition of the asset. Worth factoring into price, but not a disqualifying issue.
Federal employment — a factor to understand, not a verdict. DOGE-related federal workforce reductions have resulted in some job eliminations at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center, approximately 1.5 miles from this site. This is a real data point and worth monitoring. That said, the Greenbelt/Prince George's County corridor is anchored by a broad base of federal agencies, contractors, and government-adjacent employers — NASA Goddard is one node in a much larger ecosystem that includes the USDA Beltsville Agricultural Center, the Census Bureau HQ, the University of Maryland, and extensive federal contractor presence along I-495 and MD-193. The DC halo effect on this submarket runs both ways — it creates sensitivity to federal employment cycles, but also produces the sustained high-income, high-education demographic profile visible in the data. This is something to understand and factor in, but the overall ballast of the trade area likely renders recent developments less impactful than they might appear in isolation.

NAS Lemoore — the primary investment rationale. Naval Air Station Lemoore is the newest and largest Master Jet Base in the US Navy, home to the West Coast's entire F/A-18 Hornet and Super Hornet strike fighter force, and the primary transition base for the Navy's F-35C Joint Strike Fighter program. The installation directly employs approximately 12,000 active duty, civilian, and contractor personnel, generates over $947 million in annual economic benefit to the region, and is considered mission-critical infrastructure with long-term federal commitment. Significant F-35C squadron expansion is underway, with NAS Lemoore positioned as the Navy's West Coast F-35 hub. The base also hosts high-value military data center operations. This installation is not subject to the federal civilian workforce reductions affecting other government sites — the Navy's combat aviation mission is expanding, not contracting.
Demographic data note — Esri methodology understates the true consumer base. Esri counts NAS Lemoore barracks residents in population headcounts but excludes their income from all household-based metrics (median HHI, disposable income, net worth). Military personnel in on-base housing are classified as "group quarters" — not households — so their pay does not appear in the income data. The MRI-Simmons consumer behavior survey underlying the Chipotle MPI figures also covers civilian households only. As a result, the demographic data for this site reflects primarily the surrounding civilian community and does not capture the spending power of thousands of military personnel and their families who represent a significant share of the actual Chipotle customer base. The income and MPI figures shown below should be read as a floor, not a ceiling.

About this shortlist

These five properties were selected from a review of approximately 80 active Chipotle listings. Selection criteria included fee-simple building ownership (no ground leases), geographic fit, cap rate range, lease structure quality, demographic alignment with Chipotle's core consumer profile, and proximity to defensible demand anchors. Each site passed an initial screen; what follows is a deeper look at the factors that differentiate them.

Depreciation context

All five are fee-simple buildings qualifying for cost segregation and accelerated depreciation. California taxes passive income regardless of where the property is located — depreciation is the primary tax efficiency variable across all five sites. Year 1 accelerated deductions on a $3.7M purchase with 60% cost segregation reclassification are estimated at approximately $610K–$740K. Verify Tyler OZ eligibility separately with your CPA against specific gain timing and amount.
